Internship

During my internship at Cegeka, from 27-02-2023 to 02-06-2023, I had the privilege of working on a project focused on testing various artificial intelligence (AI) tools available for automation and integration. The primary objective was to evaluate the capabilities of three specific AI technologies: ChatGPT, the built-in AI of Palo Alto XSOAR, and other Python libraries. The project aimed to assess the effectiveness and suitability of these tools for different use cases within the organization.

Project Scope

The project scope involved researching and evaluating three AI tools: ChatGPT, Python Scikit, and Palo Alto XSOAR. After thorough evaluation, selected tools were implemented in real-world use cases, focusing on automation and integration. Meticulous documentation was maintained throughout the project, culminating in a comprehensive report. The project aimed to assess tool effectiveness and suitability for organizational AI initiatives.

automating phishing incident

The "Automating Phishing Incident" project was the primary focus of my internship at Cegeka. It aimed to revolutionize the organization's response to phishing incidents by automating the assessment process. My task was to develop an efficient system that could accurately classify phishing incidents as false positives, true positives, or benign positives.

By utilizing ChatGPT, an advanced language model, I sought to determine if it could provide consistent and reliable responses to phishing emails. This involved training ChatGPT to analyze and classify incoming phishing incidents accurately. Through a combination of machine learning techniques and data analysis, I created a system that could effectively assess the legitimacy of each incident.

automating indicator incident

One of the key use cases we explored was automating indicator incident management. Our approach involved leveraging Python to link indicator data to a remediation process, making it easier for analysts to access the relevant information. By developing a customized solution using Python, we successfully established a seamless connection between indicators and the associated research documentation. This enabled analysts to quickly access the necessary information required for assessing the impact of indicators. The integration of Python into the workflow improved efficiency and facilitated effective decision-making in the remediation process.

Additionally, we utilized ChatGPT, an advanced language model, to examine its capabilities in generating consistent plans for indicator remediation. By feeding relevant information into ChatGPT, we evaluated its ability to generate coherent and logical plans for resolving the identified indicators. This exploration allowed us to assess ChatGPT's potential in providing valuable insights and recommendations to analysts, simplifying the process of formulating remediation strategies.
